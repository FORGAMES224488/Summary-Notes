Chapter Summary: Special Entities Taxation

This chapter covers the taxation principles for Business Trusts (REITs & InvITs), Investment Funds (AIF Cat I & II), and Securitisation Trusts.

1. Business Trust (REIT / InvIT)

Structure:

  • Unit Holders invest in Business Trust (BT).
  • BT invests in Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) (Indian company, BT holds >50% shares).

Taxation of Income Passed Through to Unit Holders:

  • Dividend from SPV:
    • Exempt for BT (Sec 10(23FC)).
    • To Unit Holder:
      • Taxable if SPV paid tax under 115BAA (NR/FC: 10%, Resident: Normal Rate).
      • Exempt if SPV paid normal tax (Sec 10(23FD)).
    • TDS (Sec 194LBA): 10% if taxable (add cess for NR/FC).
  • Interest from SPV:
    • Exempt for BT (Sec 10(23FC)).
    • To Unit Holder: Taxable (NR/FC: 5%, Resident: Normal Rate).
    • TDS (Sec 194LBA): NR/FC: 5% (+cess), Resident: 10%.
  • Rental Income (REIT Owned Property):
    • Exempt for REIT (Sec 10(23FCA)).
    • To Unit Holder: Taxable at Normal Rates / Rate in Force.
    • TDS (Sec 194LBA): NR/FC: Rate in Force (+cess), Resident: 10%.
  • Nature & Proportion: Income retains its original nature and proportion when passed to Unit Holders based on distribution.

Taxation of Other Income in Business Trust's Hands:

  • LTCG Sec 112: Taxable @ 20%/12.5%.
  • STCG Sec 111A: Taxable @ 15%/20%.
  • Other Income: Taxable @ MMR (42.744% or 39% if 115BAC opted).
  • Note: LTCG Sec 112A treatment requires clarification/amendment (currently falls under Other Income by strict reading).
  • If income taxed in BT's hands, it's Exempt for Unit Holder (Sec 10(23FD)).

Other Key Points:

  • Capital Gains on Units (Sale by Unit Holder):
    • Listed + STT Paid: PoH for LT > 12 months (from 23/07/24). LTCG (112A) @ 10%/12.5% (>1.25L), STCG (111A) @ 15%/20%.
    • Unlisted/No STT: PoH for LT > 36 months. LTCG (112) @ 20%/12.5%, STCG @ Normal Rates.
  • Sponsor Concept: Initial exchange of SPV shares for BT units is not a taxable transfer. Cost & PoH of units trace back to original SPV shares.
  • Repayment of Debt (Sec 56(2)(xii)): Distributions (other than specified income) exceeding the unit's issue price are taxable as IFOS for the Unit Holder.
  • Return Filing: Mandatory for BT (Sec 139(4E)).

2. Investment Fund (AIF Cat I & II)

Structure:

  • Unit Holders invest in Investment Fund (IF).
  • IF invests in portfolio companies/ventures. (Min. investment per unit holder typically high, e.g., 1 Cr).

Taxation:

  • Investment Fund Level:
    • Only PGBP Income is Taxable. Tax Rate depends on constitution (Company/Firm: 25%/30%; Others: MMR).
    • All Other Income is Exempt.
  • Unit Holder Level:
    • All income (except PGBP) passed through is Taxable.
    • PGBP Income component is Exempt (as IF paid tax).
    • Nature & Proportion maintained.
  • Deemed Distribution: Entire income of IF (except PGBP) is taxed in Unit Holder's hands in the year earned, irrespective of actual distribution amount.
  • TDS (Sec 194LBB): NR/FC: Rate in Force (+cess), Resident: 10%.
  • Loss Treatment:
    • PGBP Loss: Retained & C/F by IF only.
    • Other Losses: Passed to Unit Holders (if they held unit for >= 12 months). Set-off/C/F by Unit Holder. If < 12 months holding, loss lapses.
  • Return Filing: Mandatory for IF (Sec 139(4F)).

3. Securitisation Trust

Structure:

  • Originator (e.g., Bank) sells receivables/assets to Securitisation Trust (ST).
  • Investors (QIBs often) invest in ST via Pass-Through Certificates etc.

Taxation:

  • Securitisation Trust Level: All income from securitisation activity is Exempt.
  • Investor Level: All income passed through is Taxable.
  • Nature & Proportion maintained.
  • Deemed Distribution: Entire income of ST is taxed in Investor's hands in the year earned, irrespective of actual distribution.
  • TDS:
    • Res. Individual/HUF: 25%.
    • Other Resident: 30%.
    • NR/FC: Rate in Force (+cess).
  • Loss Treatment: Losses from securitisation activity are borne by the Investors.

4. Reporting Requirements (Common)

  • Entities (BT, IF, ST) must provide a statement of income details (nature, proportion) to investors/unit holders by June 30th of the Assessment Year.
  • Statement to be filed with Income Tax Dept:
    • BT & ST: By November 30th of AY.
    • IF: By June 15th of AY.
