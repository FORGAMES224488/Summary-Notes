Summary: Advanced Tax, TDS & TCS

Advance Tax

  • Applicability: Mandatory if estimated tax liability ≥ ₹10,000 for the year. Applies to all assessees.
  • Calculation: Estimated Tax on Total Income - TDS/TCS - Rebate (87A) - Relief.
  • Due Dates & Cumulative % (Previous Year):
    • 15th June: 15%
    • 15th September: 45%
    • 15th December: 75%
    • 15th March: 100%
  • Sec 44AD/44ADA Assessees: Single installment: 100% by 15th March.
  • Tax paid till 31st March of PY is treated as Advance Tax. Tax paid in AY is Self-Assessment Tax.
  • Sec 210: AO can issue order to pay advance tax (based on last assessed/returned income, whichever higher) by last day of Feb of PY.

Interest Provisions (Sec 234)

  • Sec 234A: Delay in Filing ITR
    • Rate: 1% per month or part.
    • Base: Tax on Total Income (as per return/assessment) - (TDS/TCS/Adv Tax/Relief/Rebate) - Self Assessment Tax (if paid before due date - Dr. Prannoy Roy case).
    • Period: From day after Due Date till actual Filing Date (or completion of Best Judgment Assessment u/s 144 if not filed).
  • Sec 234B: Default in Payment of Advance Tax
    • Rate: 1% per month or part.
    • Base: Assessed Tax - Advance Tax Paid.
    • Period: From 1st April of AY till Date of determination of income u/s 143(1) or Assessment Date.
    • Exemption: Not levied if Advance Tax paid ≥ 90% of Assessed Tax.
  • Sec 234C: Deferment of Advance Tax Installments
    • Rate: 1% per month.
    • Period: 3 months for shortfall in 1st, 2nd, 3rd installments; 1 month for shortfall in last installment.
    • Base: Shortfall in respective installments.
    • Relaxation: No interest for 1st/2nd installment if paid ≥ 12%/36% respectively (instead of 15%/45%).
    • Note: Interest based on Returned Income, not reassessed later. Tax on Capital Gains, Winnings, etc., to be paid in remaining installments after accrual; 234C applies only for deferment in those remaining installments.
  • Sec 234D: Interest on Excess Refund Granted
    • Rate: 0.5% per month or part.
    • Base: Excess refund amount.
    • Period: From date of grant of refund till date of Regular Assessment.
  • Sec 234A(3) & 234B(3): Interest applicable in case of reassessment u/s 147 on the differential tax amount.

Fees (Sec 234)

  • Sec 234E: Late Filing of TDS/TCS Statement - Fee: ₹200 per day of default (Max: TDS/TCS amount).
  • Sec 234F: Late Filing of ITR - Fee: ₹5,000 (₹1,000 if Total Income ≤ ₹5 Lakh).
  • Sec 234G: Default by Trusts/Institutions in furnishing Donation Statements/Certificates - Fee: ₹200 per day (Max: Donation amount).
  • Sec 234H: Default in PAN-Aadhaar Linking - Fee: ₹500 (if linked 01/04/22-30/06/22), ₹1,000 (if linked on/after 01/07/22).

Interest on Refunds (Sec 244A)

  • Rate: 0.5% per month or part.
  • Period (Refund from TDS/TCS/Adv Tax):
    • ITR filed on time: From 1st April of AY to date refund granted.
    • ITR filed late: From date of filing ITR to date refund granted.
  • Period (Refund from Self-Assessment Tax): From date of tax payment or ITR filing (whichever later) to date refund granted.
  • No Interest: If refund amount < 10% of tax determined.
  • Interest received is taxable under "Income From Other Sources".

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) - Key Sections

IMPORTANT RATE CHANGES Effective 01-Oct-2024:
  • Sections 194G, 194H, 194DA, 194M, 194IB: Rate changed from 5% to 2%.
  • Section 194-O: Rate changed from 1% to 0.1%.
  • Section 194D remains at 5% (10% for domestic co.).
Check date of payment/credit carefully for applying rates.

General Rules

  • Timing: Payment or Credit, whichever is earlier.
  • Exceptions (Payment Basis Only): 192 (Salary), 192A (EPF), 194 (Dividend), 194B/BB/BA (Winnings), 194DA (LIP), 194LA (Comp. Acq.), 194N (Cash Withdrawal).
  • Rate: Generally flat rate (No HEC). Exceptions: Salary (Slab + HEC), Payments to Non-Residents (Rate in Force/DTAA + Surcharge + HEC).
  • GST Component: TDS generally not deducted on GST component if shown separately in invoice. If advance payment, TDS on full amount.
  • PAN Not Provided (Sec 206AA): Deduct at Higher of [Prescribed Rate] or [20%]. For 194-O & 194Q, Higher of [Rate] or [5%].
  • Non-Filer Payee (Sec 206AB): If Payee hasn't filed ITR for last relevant PY (where due date passed) AND aggregate TDS/TCS ≥ ₹50k in that year, deduct TDS at Higher of [Twice the Rate] or [5%]. (Not applicable on 192, 192A, 194B/BB/BA, 194LBC, 194N, 194IA, 194IB, 194M, NR without PE). If both 206AA and 206AB apply, use the higher resulting rate.

Specific Sections (Summary)

  • 192 Salary: Payer-Employer, Payee-Employee (Res/NR). Rate: Slab Rates (+HEC). Timing: Payment. Considers other income/deductions if declared (except losses other than HP loss). TCS credit also considered. No TDS on Partner's salary.
  • 192A EPF Withdrawal: Payee-Employee. Rate: 10%. Timing: Payment. Threshold: Only if payment ≥ ₹50,000 & if withdrawal is taxable (e.g., <5 yrs service from RPF, or URPF).
  • 193 Interest on Securities: Payee-Resident. Rate: 10%. Exemptions: Govt Sec (exceptions apply >₹10k for specific bonds), Listed Demat Sec, LIC/GIC payee, etc.
  • 194 Dividend: Payer-Domestic Co, Payee-Resident. Rate: 10%. Timing: Payment. Threshold: Exempt if paid to Individual ≤ ₹5,000 p.a. (other than cash). Also applies to Deemed Dividend (222a-f).
  • 194A Interest (Other): Payer-Any (HIJACKER Rule*), Payee-Resident. Rate: 10%. Thresholds: >₹5k (General), >₹40k (Bank/PO), >₹50k (Bank/PO to Senior Citizen). No TDS on Savings a/c interest, P'ship firm to partner. No TDS if payee is Bank/LIC/GIC (but applies to NBFC). Co-op Society rules apply. MACT interest >₹50k attracts TDS.
  • 194B/BB Winnings (Lottery/Horse Race): Payee-Any. Rate: 30%. Timing: Payment. Threshold: >₹10,000. Tax must be ensured before releasing prize in kind. TDS on gross winnings.
  • 194BA Online Gaming Winnings: Payee-Any. Rate: 30%. Timing: On Withdrawal & Year-End Balance. Base: Net Winnings [(Withdrawal+Closing) - (NonTax Deposit+Opening)]. Complex calculation, refer detailed notes. No TDS if Net Winnings ≤ ₹100/month (subject to conditions).
  • 194C Contract: Payer-Any (HIJACKER Rule* applies to Ind/HUF/AOP/BOI), Payee-Resident Contractor. Rates: 1% (Ind/HUF), 2% (Others). Threshold: Single >₹30k OR Agg >₹1L p.a. Covers ads, transport (transporter PAN+10 vehicle decl. exempts), catering, job work (TDS on labour if material separate). No TDS on personal contracts by Ind/HUF.
  • 194D Insurance Commission: Payee-Resident Agent. Rate: 5% (10% if payee Domestic Co.). Threshold: >₹15,000 p.a.
  • 194G Lottery Commission: Payee-Any. Rate: 5% (2% from 01-Oct-24). Threshold: >₹15,000 p.a.
  • 194H Brokerage/Commission (Other): Payer-Any (HIJACKER Rule*), Payee-Resident. Rate: 5% (2% from 01-Oct-24). Threshold: >₹15,000 p.a. Distinction between Commission (Principal-Agent) & Discount (Principal-Principal) is key (Case Laws: Stamp Vendor, Prasar Bharti, Airlines, Bharti Cellular).
  • 194DA LIP Maturity (Taxable): Payee-Resident. Rate: 5% (2% from 01-Oct-24). Timing: Payment. Threshold: Amt ≥ ₹1 Lakh. Base: Income Component (Maturity - Premiums Paid).
  • 194E NR Sportsmen/Entertainers (Non-Citizen): Payee-NR. Rate: 20% (+SC+HEC). Covers participation, ads, articles, performance fees.
  • 194I Rent: Payer-Any (HIJACKER Rule*), Payee-Resident. Rates: P&M/Eqpt: 2%; Land/Bldg/Furniture: 10%. Threshold: >₹2,40,000 p.a. per payee. Doesn't apply to refundable deposit. Applies to advance rent. Cold storage under 194C. Airport landing/parking charges under 194C (Japan Airlines). Indus Towers rent is for P&M (2%).
  • 194IA Purchase of Immovable Property (Other than Rural Agri Land): Payer(Buyer)-Any, Payee(Seller)-Resident. Rate: 1% on [Consideration or SDV, whichever >]. Threshold: TDS if Consideration OR SDV ≥ ₹50 Lakhs. Applies even with multiple buyers/sellers if property value ≥ 50L (from 01-Oct-24). TAN not required (Use PAN). Return 26QB.
  • 194IB Rent by Ind/HUF (not covered in 194I): Payer-Ind/HUF (specific), Payee-Resident. Rate: 5% (2% from 01-Oct-24). Threshold: Rent >₹50,000 p.m. Timing: Once, from last month's rent (PY or Tenancy). TAN not required (Use PAN). Return 26QC.
  • 194IC Payment under JDA: Payer(Builder)-Any, Payee(Land Owner)-Resident. Rate: 10% on monetary consideration.
  • 194J Professional/Technical Fees, Royalty, Director Fee, Non-Compete Fee: Payer-Any (HIJACKER Rule*), Payee-Resident. Rate: Generally 10%. Exceptions (2%): Technical Fee, Royalty (Film Sale/Dist/Exhib), Call Centre Fee. Threshold: >₹30k p.a. per category (Prof, Tech, Royalty, Non-Compete); No threshold for Director fee. Ind/HUF: Applies only to Prof/Tech fee if limits crossed; no TDS on personal services. Software payment generally royalty (194J). Kotak Sec case: BSE BOLT fee not technical service.
  • 194K Income from Mutual Fund/UTI: Payee-Resident. Rate: 10%. Threshold: >₹5,000 p.a.
  • 194LA Compensation on Comp. Acq. (Immovable Property excl Agri Land): Payee-Resident. Rate: 10%. Timing: Payment. Threshold: >₹2,50,000.
  • 194M Contract/Commission/Prof. Fee by Ind/HUF (not covered in C/H/J): Payer-Ind/HUF (specific), Payee-Resident. Rate: 5% (2% from 01-Oct-24). Threshold: Aggregate >₹50 Lakhs p.a. TAN not required (Use PAN). Return 26QD.
  • 194N Cash Withdrawal: Payer-Bank/PO, Payee-Any. Rate: 2% on amount >₹1 Cr p.a. (Co-op Society: >₹3 Cr). Non-Filers (last 3 yrs): Limit ₹20L; 2% on ₹20L-₹1Cr(or 3Cr); 5% >₹1Cr(or 3Cr). Exemptions: Govt, Banks, ATM operators, CRAs, APMC agents (for farmers), etc.
  • 194-O E-commerce Operator: Payer-Operator, Payee-Resident Participant. Rate: 1% (0.1% from 01-Oct-24) on Gross Sale Value. Exemption: Ind/HUF participant if Gross Sales ≤ ₹5L & PAN provided. If 194-O applies, no other TDS section. Complex rules for ONDC, discounts etc.
  • 194P Relaxation for Senior Citizen (≥75 yrs): If income only Pension + Interest (from same bank), bank computes tax & deducts under 194P. Senior Citizen need not file ITR.
  • 194Q Purchase of Goods: Payer(Buyer)-Turnover >₹10Cr last yr, Payee(Seller)-Resident. Rate: 0.1% on purchase value >₹50 Lakhs p.a. from a seller. Priority: 194-O > Other TDS/TCS (excl 1H) > 194Q > 206C(1H). Base excludes GST.
  • 194R Benefit/Perquisite (Business/Profession): Payer-Any (HIJACKER Rule*), Payee-Resident. Rate: 10%. Threshold: Aggregate value >₹20k p.a. per person. Includes incentives, sponsored trips, free samples (not 'Buy X Get Y free'). Valuation rules apply. Careful handling for kind benefits & social media influencers.
  • 195 Payment to Non-Resident/Foreign Co: Payer-Any, Payee-NR/FC. Rate: Rates in Force / DTAA Rate (beneficial one) + SC + HEC. Applies only if income taxable in India for NR/FC.

*HIJACKER Rule: Ind/HUF/AOP/BOI need not deduct TDS unless their business turnover > ₹1 Cr or professional gross receipts > ₹50 Lakhs in the preceding FY. Applies to 194A, C, H, I, J, R.

TCS (Tax Collected at Source) - Key Sections

General Rules

  • Timing: Debiting the buyer or Receipt of consideration, whichever is earlier.
  • Exceptions (Receipt Basis Only): 206C(1F) (Motor Vehicle), 206C(1H) (Sale of Goods >50L).
  • Base: Includes amount inclusive of GST (unlike TDS).
  • PAN Not Provided (Sec 206CC): Collect at Higher of [Twice the Rate] or [5%]. (Rate capped at 20% w.e.f 01-Jul-23 for 206CCA non-filer rule). For 206C(1H), if PAN not provided, rate is 1% (instead of 0.1%).
  • Non-Filer Buyer (Sec 206CCA): Similar to 206AB, Collect TCS at Higher of [Twice the Rate] or [5%]. (Exclusions apply). Max rate capped at 20%.

Specific Sections (Summary)

  • 206C(1) Sale of Specific Goods: Seller-Any (Ind/HUF if limits crossed). Buyer-Any (Excl Govt, PSC, buyer for mfg/power gen with declaration). Rates: Tendu Leaves (5%), Timber/Forest (2.5%), Liquor (1%), Scrap (1%), Minerals (Coal/Lignite/Iron Ore) (1%).
  • 206C(1C) Lease/License of Parking Lot/Toll Plaza/Mine/Quarry: Collector-Any (Ind/HUF if limits crossed). Licensee-Any (Excl PSC). Rate: 2%.
  • 206C(1F) Sale of Motor Vehicle: Seller-Any. Buyer-Any. Rate: 1%. Threshold: Sale Consideration > ₹10 Lakhs per vehicle. Retail sales only. Timing: Receipt basis.
  • 206C(1G) Foreign Remittance (LRS) & Overseas Tour Package (OTPP):
    • LRS Remittance: Collector-Authorised Dealer. Remitter-Any. Threshold: Aggregate > ₹7 Lakh p.a. Rates on excess >₹7L: 5% (Edu/Medical), 0.5% (Edu via Loan), 20% (Others).
    • OTPP Sale: Collector-Seller of Package. Buyer-Any. Rate: 5% (up to ₹7L), 20% (on amount >₹7L). *[Note: Exact interpretation/rate structure post Oct-23 changes might need verification based on specific exam year guidance, but this reflects speaker's phrasing tendency towards threshold logic].*
    • Exemptions: Govt buyers, if TDS already deducted.
  • 206C(1H) Sale of Goods (Other): Seller-Turnover >₹10Cr last yr. Buyer-Any (Exemptions: Govt, Import). Rate: 0.1%. Threshold: Consideration Received >₹50 Lakhs p.a. from a buyer. TCS on excess >₹50L. Timing: Receipt basis. Base includes GST. 194Q overrides this if applicable. Rate 1% if buyer has no PAN.

Compliance & Consequences

  • TDS Deposit Due Date: 7th of next month (30th April for March). Exception: 194IA/IB/M - 30 days from end of month.
  • TCS Deposit Due Date: 7th of next month (including for March).
  • TDS/TCS Return Due Dates (Quarterly): 31st July (Q1), 31st Oct (Q2), 31st Jan (Q3), 31st May (Q4).
  • Late Filing Fee (234E): ₹200 per day (max TDS/TCS). Penalty (271H) ₹10k-₹1L possible.
  • Interest on Late Deposit (201(1A) / TCS Rules): 1% p.m. for late deduction; 1.5% p.m. for late payment after deduction (TDS). 1% p.m. for late payment (TCS). Mandatory.
  • Assessee in Default (Sec 201): Failure to deduct or deposit TDS. Consequence: Tax demand, Interest (220), Penalty (221). Relief if payee paid tax (Form 26A). Time limit for AO order currently 7 years (becomes 6 yrs from 01-Apr-25).
  • TDS Certificate: Deductor must issue certificate to deductee.
  • Credit for TDS/TCS (Sec 199): Allowed to the deductee/collectee in the relevant assessment year.
